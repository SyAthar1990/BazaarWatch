About BazarWatch
What is BazarWatch?
BazarWatch is a freelance journalism project that addresses the issue of food adulteration in Bangladesh. Our primary function is to track mobile court raids that occur in bazars, restaurants, and food manufactories in the metropolis and provide the information for the population.
Who are we?
Zyma Islam is a data journalist with a Masters from Columbia Journalism School. Syed Bin Amir is a data scientist and electrical engineer with a Masters from Columbia School of Engineering. These two freelancers are in charge of BazarWatch.
How are we funded?
This project was born out of a offbeat discussion between two classmates and friends on the lack on any scientific study and media focus on adulterated food in Dhaka. We pitched our idea to the awesome folks at the Brown Institute for Media Innovation, and received seed funding!
